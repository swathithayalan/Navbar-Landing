Modules of the Product

Manage Location

  • The appÂ is so dynamic to add a new location at any level.

  • The location can be drilled down to any depth like Location -> Building/Block -> Floor -> House, etc.

  • By this way, the app is suitable to manage different types of job sites.

Inspection on Job Sites and Raise Issues

  • Perform inspection to log the issues on the job sites.

  • Inspection can be done from the predefined checklist items or just raising an issue in any location.

  • Provision to raise issues at any level of the building blocks.

  • The optionÂ of uploading a short video clip and images for raising any issues.

Track Issues

  • The issues can be assigned to the respective employees or vendors to fix the same.

  • Need approval for any issues raised in the job sites.

  • The issues can be filtered and categorized with different attributes of the issues.

Manage Appliances

  • Bulk upload to assign appliances to any job sites.

  • Separate option to raise issues on the appliances.

  • Facility to add new appliance type, model, etc.

  • Fetching detailed report on the job sites with the appliances installed.

Reports

  • Detailed admin report on the appliances and the issues.
